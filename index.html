All About the Neem Tree

Introduction

The Neem tree (Azadirachta indica) is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent. It has been known for centuries for its medicinal and agricultural properties.

Medicinal Uses

  • Neem leaves are used to treat skin conditions like acne and eczema.
  • Neem oil is effective against dandruff and lice.
  • Neem is a natural immunity booster and is often used in Ayurvedic medicine.

Agricultural Benefits

Neem is also used as a natural pesticide and insect repellent. Neem cake, a byproduct of neem oil extraction, is used as a fertilizer in farms and gardens.

Environmental Benefits

The Neem tree helps purify the air and prevents soil erosion. Its fast growth and deep roots make it excellent for reforestation projects.
